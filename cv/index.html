Publications

  • 2024.05.30
    CRMSP: A Semi-supervised Approach for Key Information Extraction with Class-Rebalancing and Merged Semantic Pseudo-Labeling
    Elsevier
    To address the long-tail problem in extracting information from a large amount of unlabeled text, a semi-supervised method called CRMSP is proposed, which significantly reduces labeling costs and improves the model's generalization ability. 1) The CRP module aims to solve the issue of underestimated confidence for tail classes in long-tail distributions. 2) The MSP module addresses the difficulty of achieving intra-class compactness and inter-class separability for tail classes in the unlabeled feature space.
  • 2020.10.01
    Balanced-to-unbalanced filtering magic-T based on circular patch resonator with high selectivity
    Wiley Online Library
    This Letter presents a new design of balanced-to-unbalanced (B2U) filtering magic-T based on circular patch resonator. By studying the electric field distributions of the degenerate resonant modes, the in-phase and out-of-phase specifications are achieved without additional circuits.

    Knowledge Graph-Based Visual Question Answering System
    Developed a visual question-answering system based on knowledge graphs, addressing multimodal information tasks.
    • Used ZS-VQA structure as the baseline and conducted ablation experiments on different modules. By training different feature mapping spaces (semantic space for relations, object space for supporting entities, and knowledge space for answers), the system more accurately aligned image-question pairs with answers, enhancing question-answering accuracy.
